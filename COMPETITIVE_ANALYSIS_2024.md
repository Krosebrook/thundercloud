## Executive Summary

- **Wix:** 55% market share (combined with Squarespace), 263M users, known for flexibility

- AI website generation is now table stakes

- Mobile-first design is mandatory
- SEO tools are becoming more important
- Real-time editing is expected
- Template quality > template quantity
